Stopping the debugger

To stop debugging a program, stop it by clicking on the red button in the toolbar ( ), or click on Debug > Terminate.

This will switch you back to the main OCaml perspective. Do not switch the perspective manually, as this would leave the debugger and the program running.

Note: Do NOT click on the terminate button in the console, under normal circumstances, as this would kill the debugged process directly, instead of asking the debugger to terminate.
